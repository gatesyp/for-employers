How DeepHire Works

The most intuitive way to source and hire the best culture fit.

1 Post Your Profile

Fill out your profile online, and then download the app to create & upload authentic videos. Add interview questions to save time and ensure that you see how candidates answer questions, on video.

2 Attract the right fit.

As soon as your profile is posted, we broadcast it to an audience of job seekers. They get to watch and interact with your video profiles, and our artificial intelligence automatically identifies which candidates are the most interested - automatically engaging them with your interview questions.

3 Receive Great Candidates

84% of job seekers say that culture fit is their number one priority when choosing a new job. When a candidate is identified as a good fit for your company, you recieve their resume and video profile on your Candidate Dashboard.

4 Review Candidates

You will only be presented with the best candidates possible. The best candidates are those that already fit into your culture and are excited for the first day at work - before you even interview them.

5 Interview & Hire

Scheduling interviews is annoying and a headache - so let us do it! Our software helps find a good time for both your team and the candidate. Having seen your content, the candidate will already be eager to talk with you.
